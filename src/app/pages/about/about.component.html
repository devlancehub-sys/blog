Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves a space to unwind, laugh, and connect through voice. TalkyMate brings together hosts and callers for light-hearted entertainment — no video pressure, just real conversations that feel personal and fun.

What We Offer

TalkyMate is designed purely for entertainment through voice calls. Browse available hosts, start a call in seconds, and enjoy conversations on topics you love — from casual chat to playful banter.

  • Instant voice calls with verified hosts
  • Safe, private, and entertainment-focused experience
  • Simple wallet system to manage call minutes
  • Call history so you can reconnect with your favourites

Entertainment Only

TalkyMate is strictly for entertainment purposes. We do not offer dating, matchmaking, or professional counselling services. Our platform is a place to relax, have fun, and enjoy voice-based conversations in a respectful environment.

Our Values

Safety First

We monitor activity, enforce community guidelines, and give users tools to report inappropriate behaviour. Your comfort and privacy matter to us.

Respect Always

Every call should be enjoyable for both sides. We expect all users and hosts to treat each other with kindness and respect.

Fun by Design

From the moment you open the app to the end of your call, everything is built to keep the experience easy, engaging, and entertaining.
