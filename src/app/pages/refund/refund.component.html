Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: {{ site.lastUpdated }}. This policy explains refunds and cancellations for TalkyMate wallet recharges and services.

1. Nature of Service

TalkyMate provides digital wallet credits for voice-call entertainment services. Once credits are added to your wallet after a successful payment, they are considered delivered digital goods.

2. Wallet Recharge Refunds

Refunds for wallet recharges may be considered in the following cases:

  • Payment was deducted but credits were not added to your wallet
  • Duplicate payment for the same recharge order
  • Technical error on TalkyMate's side during payment processing

Refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of the transaction to {{ site.supportEmail }} with your registered phone number, transaction ID, and payment date.

3. Non-Refundable Items

The following are generally not eligible for refund:

  • Credits already used for completed voice calls
  • Partially used wallet balance
  • Account termination due to violation of Terms & Conditions
  • Dissatisfaction with a host or call experience (subject to review on case-by-case basis)

4. Call Cancellations

If a call ends due to network issues on either side, credits are charged only for the duration of the active call. If you believe you were incorrectly charged, contact support with call details within 48 hours.

5. Refund Processing

Approved refunds are processed to the original payment method within 7–10 business days, depending on your bank or payment provider. Cashfree and banking partners may take additional time to reflect the refund in your account.

6. Cancellation of Recharge

Once a wallet recharge payment is successful and credits are credited, the transaction cannot be cancelled. Please verify the recharge amount before completing payment.

7. Disputes

For payment disputes, contact us first at {{ site.supportEmail }}. We will investigate and respond within 3–5 business days.
