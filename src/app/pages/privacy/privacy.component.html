1. Introduction

TalkyMate ("we", "us", "our") operates a voice-call entertainment platform and website. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in accordance with applicable Indian laws.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use TalkyMate:

  • Account Information: Phone number, display name, profile photo, and account preferences
  • Payment Information: Transaction IDs, payment amounts, and wallet recharge history (payment card details are processed securely by Cashfree and are not stored by TalkyMate)
  • Usage Data: Call duration, timestamps, host/caller IDs, and app interaction data
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version, and IP address
  • Contact Information: Email address and messages submitted through our contact form

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide voice-call entertainment services
  • Process wallet recharges and payment transactions
  • Manage your account and wallet balance
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Enforce community guidelines and prevent fraud or misuse
  • Improve our app, website, and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Payment Processing

Payments on TalkyMate are processed through Cashfree, a third-party payment gateway. When you make a payment, your payment information is transmitted directly to Cashfree. TalkyMate does not store your full card or UPI credentials. We receive only transaction confirmation data necessary to credit your wallet.

5. Voice Calls & Recordings

TalkyMate voice calls are live and are not recorded by default. We do not store the audio content of your conversations unless required by law. Call duration and billing metadata may be logged for service delivery and dispute resolution.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Payment processors (e.g., Cashfree) for transaction processing
  • Cloud hosting and infrastructure providers
  • Law enforcement or regulatory authorities when required by law

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/SSL), access controls, and secure payment processing. While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Data Retention

We retain your account and transaction data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your profile information
  • Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Withdraw consent for non-essential data processing
  • Report privacy concerns to our support team
